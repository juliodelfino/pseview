Metro Alliance Holdings & Equities Corp. (MAH), formerly Marsman & Company, Inc., was incorporated on October 15, 1929 as a management and trading company. The Company and its subsidiaries are involved in the manufacture of chemicals and contract logistics. The Securities and Exchange Commission approved the Company&#39;s change in name to the present one on October 11, 1999. In 2007, the Company&#39;s interest in certain subsidiaries previously engaged in the importation and distribution of polypropylene resin and pharmacy management has ceased operations.<br/><br/>MAH&#39;s subsidiary is Metro Combined Logistics Solutions, Inc., which is in the business of contract logistics and supply chain management services, including third party warehousing and distribution, consultancy and project management and value added services to customers throughout the Philippines.<br/><br/>The Company&#39;s non-operating subsidiaries include Consumer Products Distribution Services, Inc.; FEZ-EAC Holdings, Inc.; Zuellig Distributors, Inc.; and Asia Healthcare, Inc.<br/><br/>Source: SEC Form 17-A (2015)<br/>
