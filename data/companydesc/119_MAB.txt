Manila Mining Corporation (MA) was incorporated on May 20, 1949 to engage in the mining and exploration of metals. MA started its mining operations in Placer, Surigao del Norte in 1981. Until 2001, MA was producing gold bullion through a carbon in-pulp plant and copper concentrates containing gold, silver and copper from MA&#39;s copper flotation plant. In July 2001, MA suspended its mining and milling operations pending receipt of the permit from the Department of Environment and Natural Resources to further elevate and operate MA&#39;s tailings pond number seven. On January 29, 2007, MA obtained the release of its Renewed Exploration Permit covering 2,462.9 hectares in Surigao del Norte.<br/><br/>MA has two existing mining lease contracts with the government located in Surigao del Norte and three Mineral Production Sharing Agreements covering Surigao del Norte and Agusan del Norte. MA is an affiliate of Lepanto Consolidated Mining Company. MA has one subsidiary, the Kalaya-an Copper-Gold Resources, Inc. of which MA owns 95%, while the remaining is owned by Philex Mining Corporation.<br/><br/>As of December 31, 2015, the Company has no new products or services.<br/><br/>Source: SEC Form 17-A (2015)<br/>
