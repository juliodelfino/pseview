Da Vinci Capital Holdings, Inc. (DAVIN) was incorporated as Mariwasa Manufacturing, Inc. on November 5, 1953 to primarily engage in the manufacture of ceramic tiles. On July 2, 2008, the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) approved the change in corporate name to Mariwasa Siam Holdings, Inc. (Mariwasa Siam) and change in its primary purpose from manufacturing to that of an investment holding company. On April 26, 2013, the SEC approved the change in name of Mariwasa Siam Holdings, Inc. to the present one.<br/><br/>The manufacturing operations of DAVIN were concentrated in its wholly-owned subsidiary, Mariwasa-Siam Ceramics, Inc. (MSCI). However, on March 15, 2012, following the implementation of the Company&#39;s reorganization plan, MSCI ceased to be a subsidiary of DAVIN.<br/><br/>As of December 31, 2015, DAVIN is a shell company without operation.<br/><br/>Source: SEC Form 17-A (2015)
