First Gen Corporation (FGEN) was incorporated and registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission on December 22, 1998. FGEN and its subsidiaries are involved in the power generation business. FGEN is a clean and renewable Independent Power Producer (IPP), with an installed capacity of 2,957 MW as of December 31, 2015. <br/><br/>The Company owns power plants which utilize natural gas, geothermal, wind, hydro and solar power, all of which are operational and majority-owned and controlled by the Company through its subsidiaries. These subsidiaries include First Gen Renewables, Inc.; First Gen Energy Solutions, Inc.; First Gen Premier Energy Corp.; First Gen Prime Energy Corporation; First Gen Visayas Energy, Inc.; and Northern Terracotta Power Corp. <br/><br/>As of December 31, 2015, First Philippine Holdings Corporation directly and indirectly owns 66.2% of the common shares of FGEN and 100% of FGEN&#39;s voting preferred shares. Lopez, Inc. is the ultimate parent company of FGEN. <br/><br/>Source: SEC Form 17-A (2015)
