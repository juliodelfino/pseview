NiHAO Mineral Resources International, Inc. (NI), formerly Magnum Holdings, Inc. (MHI), was incorporated on July 9, 1975 under the name Summit Minerals, Inc. On December 10, 1993, the Company changed its primary purpose to that of a holding company and on February 24, 1994, changed its corporate name to MHI. On June 28, 2007, the SEC approved the change in corporate name to the present one and the primary purpose to that of a mining company. Presently, NI is engaged in exploring, developing, and operating nickel properties.<br/><br/>As a mining company, NI&#39;s primary purpose is to carry on the business of mining, milling, concentrating, converting, smelting, preparing for market, manufacturing, buying, selling, exchanging, and otherwise producing and dealing in nickel, chromite, and other kinds of ores, metal, minerals, and by-products.<br/><br/>As of December 31, 2015, the Company has three mining claims located in the provinces of Zambales, Misamis Oriental, and Masbate. The Company&#39;s subsidiaries consist of Mina Tierra Gracia, Inc., Bountiful Geomines, Inc., and Oregalore, Inc.<br/><br/>Source: SEC Form 17-A (2015)
