TKC Metals Corporation (T), formerly TKC Steel Corporation, was originally incorporated on November 28, 1996 as SQL* Wizard, Inc. and engaged in providing computer training, consulting services and selling of software licenses and other related information solution services. On February 28, 2007, the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) approved the change in the Company&#39;s primary purpose from information technology to that of a holding company. On October 26, 2015, the SEC approved the Company&#39;s change in corporate name to the present one.    <br/><br/>T is engaged in the business of manufacturing and distributing steel products through its two subsidiaries namely, Treasure Steelworks Corporation (TSC), and Zhangzhou Stronghold Steel Works Co. Ltd (ZZS). TSC manufactures steel billets, which in turn become the raw materials of downstream steel products such as bars, wire rods and sections. ZZS manufactures various types of steel pipes and undertakes the distribution of its products in China and other export markets.<br/><br/>Source: SEC Form 17-A (2015)
