Alterra Capital Partners, Inc. (ALT), formerly iRipple, Inc. (RPL), was incorporated on November 21, 2000 to establish, operate, develop, manage and provide software solution projects and related businesses as well as to engage in trading of computer hardware and software products. RPL started commercial operations in January 2002.<br/><br/>On December 16, 2014, the Board approved the sale of all of RPL&#39;s assets to Movmento, Inc. in preparation for a contemplated change in primary purpose of the Company. The disposed assets include motor vehicles, office equipment as well as trademarks, copyright and other intellectual property rights pertaining to the Company&#39;s software products and all of the rights and interests in its existing contracts.<br/><br/>On March 12, 2015, 10 shareholders of RPL entered into a share purchase agreement and sold their 89.49% interest in the Company in favor of two individual investors. In May, RPL applied to amend the change in corporate name to the present one as well as the change in primary purpose to that of a holding company. The Securities and Exchange Commission approved the amendment of the Amended Articles of Incorporation on October 29, 2015.<br/><br/>Source: SEC Form 17-A (2015)
